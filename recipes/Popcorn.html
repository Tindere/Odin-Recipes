Pizza

Description

Corn pop may have been a bad dude, but popcorn is delicious.

Ingredients

    Ingredients
  • Corn Kernels
    • Necessities
  • A microwave

Steps

  1. Place Kernels into a microwave
  2. Heat the kernels until they stop popping
  3. Wait for them to cool down
  4. Optional: Add butter and flavoured powder if necessary
  5. Enjoy
